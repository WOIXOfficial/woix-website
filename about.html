OUR STORY

Connecting Businesses.
Creating Opportunities.

WOIX was established with a clear objective—to simplify international sourcing by connecting global buyers with trusted Indian manufacturers across multiple industries.

We believe international trade is built on trust, transparency and long-term partnerships. Every supplier in our network is evaluated for quality, production capability and reliability before being introduced to our global clients.

Our expertise spans decorative laminates, building materials, engineering products, agriculture and customized sourcing solutions, enabling buyers to expand confidently into international markets.

Beyond sourcing, WOIX provides export documentation, logistics coordination, compliance support and market intelligence, making international trade simple, transparent and efficient.
